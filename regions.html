Regions of Teyvat

You have arrived in Teyvat — a fantasy world where the seven elements flow and converge.
In the distant past, the Archons granted mortals unique elemental abilities. With the help of such powers, people formed a bountiful homeland out of the wilderness. However, 500 years ago, the collapse of an ancient civilization turned the universe upside down...
Though the calamity the world suffered has ceased, peace has yet to be restored.

Mondstadt

A city of freedom that lies in the northeast of Teyvat.
From amongst mountains and wide-open plains, carefree breezes carry the scent of dandelions — a gift from the Anemo God, Barbatos — across Cider Lake to Mondstadt, which sits on an island in the middle of the lake.

Landmarks

Cider Lake

A natural freshwater lake whose clear, cool waters have remained unchanged for ages, becoming the famed source of Mondstadt’s ciders, wines, and its iconic identity.

Falcon Coast

Stretching from Windrise to the sea, Falcon Coast — together with Cape Oath — forms a sheltered bay east of Mondstadt, known for its long shoreline, shallow waters, and the falcons that glide above its white sands.

Cape Oath

At Mondstadt’s southeastern edge lies a cape bordering Galesong Hill, famed for a lovers’ legend and beloved by young couples, offering clear views of Windrise and stunning sunrises and sunsets thanks to its elevated terrain.

Dawn Winery

Southwest of Mondstadt lies a historic winery that has produced the region’s renowned wines and ciders for generations, using locally grown grapes and distributing its yearly batches both within Mondstadt.

Springvale

South of Mondstadt lies the sleepy lakeside village of Springvale, home to skilled hunters whose fresh meats supply the city’s restaurants and draw visitors seeking its rustic charm and exceptional local dishes.

Thousand Winds Temple

Northeast of Mondstadt lie ancient temple ruins once dedicated to the Anemo God, now weathered by centuries yet still etched with faint carvings and the old maxim, “Wind brings the seeds of tales, time allows them to take root.”

Liyue

A bountiful harbor that lies in the east of Teyvat.
Mountains stand tall and proud alongside the stone forest, that together with the open plains and lively rivers make up Liyue's bountiful landscape, which shows its unique beauty through each of the four seasons. Just how many gifts from the Geo God lie in wait amongst the rocks of Liyue's mountains?

Landmarks

Liyue Harbor

The largest market port in Teyvat, drives unmatched seafaring trade and hosts the must-see annual Lantern Rite with thousands of lanterns lighting up the night sky.

Mt. Tianheng

The mountains west of Liyue Harbor serve as a natural shield, with ancient ruins of walls and forts marking historic battles and early mining operations.

Dihua Marsh

North of Liyue, is a lush wetland named for its silvergrass, forming a vibrant ecosystem and serving as a key rest stop for merchant caravans, with the iconic Wangshu Inn at its heart.

Wangshu Inn

Perched atop a massive stone pillar in Dihua Marsh, offers travelers a trading hub with breathtaking views of Mt. Qingce and Jueyun Karst, and is rumored to hide a mysterious secret.

Qingce Village

Qingce Village, tucked among hills and bamboo forests at northern Liyue, features crescent-moon water paddies in the valleys that shimmer with colors as the wind stirs.

Jueyun Karst

The mist-shrouded peaks in northwest Liyue are famed for stunning views and rumored Adepti homes, though dangerous paths make them best admired from afar at Wangshu Inn.

Inazuma

An Isolated Archipelago Far East of Teyvat
Overcome endless thunderstorms and set foot on the islands of red maple and cherry blossoms. On winding shores and towering cliffs, and in forests and mountains full of secrets, witness the Eternity pursued by Her Excellency, the Almighty Narukami Ogosho.

Landmarks

Ritou

During the Sakoku Decree, entering Inazuma via Ritou requires a Travel Permit; without it, travelers glimpse only the village’s red maples and green roofs.

Inazuma City

Inazuma City, the bustling heart of Inazuma, is home to most of its population, traditional shops, the Tenryou Commission, and Tenshukaku, where the Raiden Shogun rules.

Chinju Forest

Chinju Forest, at the foot of Mt. Yougou, is a tranquil, mysterious forest filled with Tanuki statues, scattered torii gates, hidden shrines, and rumored mischievous spirits.

Grand Narukami Shrine

The Grand Narukami Shrine sits on Mt. Yougou, where the Sacred Sakura blooms endlessly and worshippers pass through torii gates to seek blessings.

Musoujin Gorge

The Musou Isshin Rift on eastern Yashiori Island, formed from the Electro Archon’s slaying of a giant serpent, still bears the serpent’s remains and awakens Tatarigami across the island.

Serpent's Head

The slain serpent of Watatsumi Omikami lingers on Yashiori Island, its power fading under storms and rain brought by Tatarigami.

Sumeru

The city of scholars located in the west-central part of Teyvat.
A fantastical nation of both lush rainforest and barren desert, where countless fruits of wisdom grow and are buried. Whether Travelers travel from afar through the forest to reach the academy city or delve deep into the desert to discover the historical ruins of the red desert, a wealth of valuable knowledge awaits them here.

Landmarks

Gandharva Ville

The city above the forest. "The city of wisdom is ours, as is the forest of ignorance. Atop the tree lies the remnants of the kingdom of lost dreams."

Pardis Dhyai

The garden of deep thought. Countless flowers lie within the garden, each more lovely than the most beautiful crystalfly. This is the paradise of people of goodwill.

Caravan Ribat

The frontier beneath the high wall. The mighty wall defends against the searing sandstorms but has also blocked the spread of wisdom's blessing.

Port Ormos

The harbor betwixt the two trees. “Bah! The sages’lectures are dreadfully boring. Why not sip some wine with floral honey and sail across the seven seas instead?”

Sumeru City

The root of all wisdom on earth. Beneath the lush Divine Tree, the academy city’s sages have gathered all knowledge. Welcome to Sumeru, traveler seeking answers.

Mawtiyima Forest

The forest of enormous fluorescent mushrooms. Here, giant mushroom caps construct a canopy of light, obscuring the sun and the moon.

Apam Woods

The forest of a rain without end. The towering trees reach for the sky freely. Perhaps one day its branches will touch the stars. But under their canopy, the rain has never ceased.

Devantaka Mountain

The mountain that saw the fall of the demons. Hidden in the greenery lies a colossal machine, its light extinguished. Circular holes like eyes silently watch the passage of time.

Fontaine

A terrestrial sea in the center of Teyvat.
Following the direction of pure currents, crossing wilderness, the depths of the forests and vastness of the sea of sand, arriving at the origin of all the waters of the continent. At the top of the waterfall, in the depths of the capital atop the terrestrial sea... a story that has never been heard, a legend that has been forgotten, like a lost kingdom sunken beneath the waves, yearning for a bard to sing its drowned songs.

Landmarks

Court of Fontaine

Fontaine, the city above all waters, is ruled from the towering Palais Mermonia, where law and order ensure the people’s lives flow as steadily as the aquabus.

Elynas

Its enormous skeleton melds with the mountains, leaving onlookers to wonder why it came and if, as legends say, a home lies within its body.

Romaritime Harbor

The roaring waterfall masks the harbor below, but aboard the Clementine Line, the journey to Hydro’s heart makes the wait feel insignificant.

Elton Trench

Its deceptively bright waters hide deep shipwrecks, and the haunted legends make it an ideal refuge for those seeking secrecy.

Opera Epiclese

A grand opera house in Erinnyes, symbolizing trials and judgment, where the comedic, tragic, real, and fantastical all share the stage.

West Slopes of Mont Automnequi

Towers over the Poisson Tunnel — the FR&I project that made the aquabus lines possible.

Salacia Plain

A mysterious power from the statue blesses you with the Transoceanic Sourcewater, letting you swim unhindered while following the currents and the songs of Blubberbeasts.

Natlan

The nation of oaths situated in the western regions of Teyvat.
Guided by the flame, you pass through lengthy gorges and valleys to reach the land where the torches blaze, where all fires converge. Upon the blazing fields where Saurians roam, witness the end of the ancient war alongside the valiant warriors who resist the darkness.

Landmarks

Basin of Unnumbered Flames

In the name of victory and honor, may the fires of conflict be ignited. The fervor with which all souls burn shall become fuel for the everlasting Sacred Flame.

Coatepec Mountain

The settlement of the tribe who live alongside the Yumkasaurs. Their ancestors suspended giant trees here with ropes and grappling hooks, "Scions of the Canopy."

Tequemecan Valley (1)

Home of the tribe who live alongside Tepetlisaurs, the Children of Echoes. The clanging of artisans' hammers and the tapping of gem hunters testing the rocks constantly echo through the air.

Tequemecan Valley (2)

Long ago, a sage mastered Phlogiston Engraving, using cliffs as canvases for vibrant murals—giving rise to the Natlan graffiti we know today.

Toyac Springs

The tribe who lives alongside the Koholasaurs, the "People of the Springs," renowned for their hot springs and relaxing music. This is the most popular among visitors from afar.

Ancestral Temple

In an age when the earth rejected seeds, flowers withered, and people could no longer rely on the land for life, the ancestors of that era sought guidance from the gods of the night.

Sulfurous Veins

Giant Solid Phlogiston Nails called "Shadow Pins," said to be forged from the flesh and blood of the Children of Echoes’ warriors and hung here to ward off the ancient shadows below.

The Upper Sanctum

A hidden realm of purity sealed in mid-air, once a place where a merciful sage taught secret arts — now concealed for reasons long forgotte

Firethief's Secret Isle

"O puny people, o power-hungry race — what price are you willing to pay? What sacrifice will you make for that which you covet?"

Chamber of Night's Trial

Through the blaze of battle alone can these unquiet Nightsouls be stilled to slumber!

Nod Krai

The moon-blessed land located in the northwestern regions of Teyvat.
Follow the silver thread spun by the new moon, traverse biting gales and desolate tundras as the snow-kissed lakes, fractured like mirrors, reflect their ageless, glacial radiance. The days are bitterly short, and the night stretches on like a dark, yawning maw. But even then, the light and song of the Welkin Moon will dance gently across the faces of visiting travelers. Up at ye towers, and let your lanterns blaze into the darkness! Until the day comes... when true moonlight bathes this land once more.

Landmarks

Border Town

This town sits like a ship run aground on the world's edge — a place where drifters gather to scavenge fragments of elysium from the wreckage of history and fabricated pasts.

Clink-Clank Krumkake Craftshop

Countless mechanical creations, both large and small, are brought into existence here!

Fortress of Steel

This leviathan of white iron and black steel devours all it sees—not for plunder, but for the power to survive the coming calamity.

Secret Research

A massive vessel containing captured moonlight.

Echoes of the Far North

"O Moon, benevolent mistress, whither dost thou lead us?"

Lies of the Faithless

"We are all tainted by sin... How could we ever hope to behold the moonlight in its purest form?"

Silent Shallows

Steel structures rise from this pink geological layer like jarring notes in a melody. Mysterious lifeforms once dwelled here — mindless and voiceless, they lived without sound and vanished in silence.

New World's Prelude

Just as the frontier folk weave their prayers into verse beneath the moonlight, the craftsmen of the north temper defiant ambition into steel as cold and bright as moonlight.

Neonate Aberration

They defined life with human arrogance, yet could not define the end of their own.

Iron Whale's Dream

Its massive gears have long fallen silent, its steel veins reduced to rust. But when someone finally summons it from slumber, the great beast wakes and breathes toward the stars once more.

Snezhnaya

The frost-bound dominion resting at the farthest reaches of northern Teyvat.
Walk through fields of eternal snowfall and winds sharpened by steel, where frozen rivers lie still beneath layers of ancient ice, and the land sleeps under a blanket of unbroken winter. The sun rises pale and distant, giving way to nights that linger like a cold vow whispered to the world. Yet in that endless winter, the glow of braziers and the disciplined rhythm of marching steps echo across the capital, carrying the will of the Tsaritsa to every corner of her realm. Raise your eyes to the towering citadel, its spires crowned with frost — for the day shall come… when her unwavering resolve reshapes this land anew.
