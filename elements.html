Elements

The seven elements shape the world of Teyvat. Each element is linked to an Archon, a nation, and a unique set of reactions that define combat and exploration.

ANEMO

Anemo represents freedom, movement, and the natural flow of the wind across Teyvat. It focuses on crowd control and swirl-based reactions that enhance other elements.

  • Region: Mondstadt
  • Archon: Barbatos
  • Oculus: Anemoculus
  • Gem: Vayuda Turquoise
Trivia

Anemo is the first element the Traveler resonates with at the beginning of the story. Mondstadt’s culture of freedom reflects the Anemo Archon’s passive and hands-off leadership.

GEO

Geo symbolizes stability and the unbreakable strength of the earth. Its mechanics revolve around shields, durability, and fortified structures.

  • Region: Liyue
  • Archon: Morax
  • Oculus: Geoculus
  • Gem: Prithiva Topaz
Trivia

Geo’s reactions are more defensive, making it great for survival and consistent damage. Liyue’s traditions and architecture are shaped heavily by the Geo Archon’s emphasis on contracts and order.

ELECTRO

Electro channels energy, ambition, and the pursuit of eternity. Its reactions revolve around continuous damage and rapid energy generation.

  • Region: Inazuma
  • Archon: Beelzebul
  • Oculus: Electroculus
  • Gem: Vajrada Amethyst
Trivia

The symbol of the element Electro is likely based on Mitsudomoe, a symbol often found in traditional Japanese heraldry.
Electro’s mechanics reward fast-paced combat and skill usage. Inazuma’s strict laws reflect the Archon’s desire to maintain an unchanging eternal nation.

DENDRO

Dendro represents life, wisdom, and the natural cycle of growth and decay. Its reactions are some of the most powerful, creating chain effects that amplify multiple team roles.

  • Region: Sumeru
  • Archon: Buer
  • Oculus: Dendroculus
  • Gem: Nagadus Emerald
Trivia

Dendro is the newest playable element, introduced with Sumeru. Its reactions focus on chain effects that make it one of the strongest support elements in the game.

HYDRO

Hydro symbolizes adaptability, healing, and justice that flows through society. Its reactions create powerful healing and damage combos that fit many team styles.

  • Region: Fontaine
  • Archon: Focalors
  • Oculus: Hydroculus
  • Gem: Varunada Lazurite
Trivia

Hydro is central to Fontaine’s court system, where the law defines every aspect of life. Its connection to the Primordial Sea makes it one of the most ancient and mysterious elements in the lore.

PYRO

Pyro embodies passion, strength, and relentless destructive power. Its reactions often produce explosive damage, making it one of the most aggressive elements.

  • Region: Natlan
  • Archon: Haborym
  • Oculus: Pyroculus
  • Gem: Agnidus Agate
Trivia

Pyro has the largest number of strong DPS characters due to its high reaction multipliers. Natlan’s fiery culture and warrior traditions reflect the untamed nature of the Pyro element.

CRYO

Cryo represents clarity, endurance, and emotional restraint. Its reactions allow both high burst damage and powerful crowd control.

  • Region: Snezhnaya
  • Archon: Tsaritsa
  • Oculus: Cryoculus
  • Gem: Shivada Jade
Trivia

Cryo pairs extremely well with Hydro, enabling freeze teams that dominate enemy movement. Snezhnaya’s Cryo Archon leads the Fatui, making Cryo politically powerful throughout Teyvat.
