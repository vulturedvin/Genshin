Exploration

Exploration in Teyvat goes beyond walking—it's about discovering hidden paths, solving challenges, and interacting with the environment. Here, we highlight the core mechanics that make traversing this world an unforgettable journey, without revealing specific locations or events.

The Open World

Teyvat's expansive landscapes invite free exploration, where every hill, forest, and coastline holds potential discoveries. Travelers can roam at will, uncovering vistas, wildlife, and interactive elements that bring the world to life.

Domains

Special areas known as Domains offer challenging encounters and rewards. These enclosed spaces test skills and provide valuable items, encouraging repeated visits to master their secrets and reap the benefits.

Puzzles and Mechanisms

Throughout Teyvat, ancient mechanisms and puzzles await clever minds. Solving them requires observation, logic, and sometimes elemental abilities, rewarding explorers with access to new areas and treasures.

Climbing and Traversal

Vertical exploration is key in Teyvat's diverse terrain. Climbing walls, gliding across chasms, and using environmental features allow adventurers to reach heights and hidden spots that others might miss.

Resources and Gathering

The world is rich with resources essential for crafting and progression. From ores to herbs, gathering requires exploration skills and timing, ensuring that every journey contributes to a traveler's growth.
